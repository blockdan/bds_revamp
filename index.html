Brown Data Science

About

We are a brand new student group on Brown's campus called Brown Data Science. Our club is dedicated to helping students interested in data science or machine learning gain more real world experience by acting as a platform for students to work together on projects analyzing interesting data sets.

We plan to create a data science community for undergrads by working with Brown's Data Science Initiative, taking advantage of Brown's computing resources, and working with professors to create independent studies for projects. We will also be hosting a DataFest (similar to a hackathon but data science specific!) this semester.
